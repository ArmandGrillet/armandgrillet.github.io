Armand Grillet

Software engineer at Mesosphere

Hi there! I'm Armand, a French developer who loves coding interesting projects, learning how cloud computing works and creating playlists on Spotify.

I work at Mesosphere in Hamburg where I develop the tools used to test DC/OS at scale. I also run and monitor applications that use Marathon, Cassandra, and Kafka on top of the latest versions of DC/OS and Apache Mesos to test new features and report issues.

I have previously followed an International Master’s Programme with a major in Cloud Computing and Services and a minor in Innovation & Entrepreneurship. First year in Rennes (Université de Rennes 1), second year in Berlin (Technische Universität Berlin).

During this program I have written a master thesis for the SNET department of TU Berlin and done multiple internships, including one for the INRIA.

I like to write on Medium, e.g. Comparison of Container Schedulers and Parallel Self-Tuning Spectral Clustering on Apache Spark (a short version of my master thesis).

Most of my projects are on GitHub, feel free to contact me by email or using LinkedIn.
